Crimson Desert HQ

Crimson Desert HQ is a community-driven fan wiki and strategy portal for Crimson Desert, the action RPG by Pearl Abyss. It centralizes guides, databases, and walkthroughs into a single hub — updated daily, with 100+ guides, and not affiliated with Pearl Abyss.

Key Features

  • Boss Guides - Detailed encounter strategies and boss walkthroughs
  • Weapon Database - Weapon listings with stats, locations, and upgrade info
  • Mount Collection - Mount entries with stats, types, and acquisition details
  • Beginner Tips - 57+ guides to get new players started
  • Builds & Loadouts - Optimized character builds and equipment recommendations
  • Latest News - Patch notes and community updates
  • Search - Quick access to guides, bosses, and weapons

What Makes It Different

A centralized, community-focused knowledge hub updated daily — built by players, for players, rather than official developer documentation.
